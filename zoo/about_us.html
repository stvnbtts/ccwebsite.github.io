About Us

West Arvada Zoo

West Arvada Zoo is a zoo that cares about its animals. We can guarantee that our animals are getting the best care, and are rescued from environments where they couldn't survive on their own. Our zoo has many different animals from exotic animals to animals that you might have seen in your own backyard. Lions, tigers, and bears along with goats, sheep, and pigs, and a variety of squirrels that we did not let in. We also have our famous wildcat, so feel free to post a picture on social media when you go and see him!

by Steven Batts
